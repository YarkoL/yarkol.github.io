This is an example how I usually submit comics scripts, if no particular format has been specified. I sketch lightly the panel contents to give an idea of the picture composition and balloon placement. This enables the artist to work easily and swiftly.

This little continuity of strips was originally submitted to Rovio, but was turned down, since at the time they were more interested in one-shot gag strips. So it is presented here as "fan art".
